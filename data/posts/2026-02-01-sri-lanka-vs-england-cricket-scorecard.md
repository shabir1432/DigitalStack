## How to Read the Sri Lanka vs England Cricket Scorecard

Understanding a cricket scorecard might seem daunting at first, but it's actually quite simple once you know what to look for. Here’s a breakdown of the key elements:

*   **Batsman:** The name of the player who was batting.
*   **How Out:** How the batsman was dismissed (e.g., bowled, caught, lbw).
*   **Runs:** The number of runs scored by the batsman.
*   **Balls:** The number of balls faced by the batsman.
*   **4s:** The number of boundaries (four runs) hit by the batsman.
*   **6s:** The number of sixes (six runs) hit by the batsman.
*   **Extras:** Additional runs awarded to the batting team due to wides, no-balls, leg byes, or byes.
*   **Total:** The total number of runs scored by the team.
*   **Bowler:** The name of the bowler.
*   **Overs:** The number of overs bowled by the bowler.
*   **Maidens:** The number of overs bowled by the bowler where no runs were scored.
*   **Runs:** The number of runs conceded by the bowler.
*   **Wickets:** The number of wickets taken by the bowler.

By understanding these elements, you can easily analyze the performance of each player and the overall dynamics of the match.
