## What is Stuttgart FC?

What is **Stuttgart FC** and why is it so beloved? VfB Stuttgart, officially Verein für Bewegungsspiele Stuttgart 1893 e. V., is a German professional football club based in Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg. Founded in 1893, the club has a long and storied history, marked by periods of great success and challenging times. They play their home games at the Mercedes-Benz Arena. The club's colors are white and red.

Stuttgart FC has a dedicated following, known for their unwavering support. The club has won the German championship five times and the DFB-Pokal three times.
