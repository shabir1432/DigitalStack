*   **Carbon Footprint Analysis:** Carbon footprint analysis measures the greenhouse gas emissions associated with a product, service, or activity.
*   **Cost-Benefit Analysis:** Cost-benefit analysis compares the costs and benefits of sustainable initiatives, including environmental and social benefits.
*   **Tracking Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):** Monitoring KPIs such as energy consumption, waste generation, and water usage.
