## Instagram Login: Troubleshooting Common Issues

Having trouble with your **instagram login**? You're not alone. Here are some common issues and how to fix them:

*   **Forgotten Password:** Use the "Forgot Password?" link on the login screen to reset your password via email or phone number.
*   **Account Hacked:** If you suspect your account has been hacked, immediately change your password and report the issue to **instagram** support.
*   **Two-Factor Authentication Issues:** If you're having trouble with two-factor authentication, ensure your phone number is up-to-date and try using backup codes.
*   **Account Disabled:** If your account has been disabled, follow the appeal process outlined by **instagram**. This usually involves verifying your identity.
*   **Can't remember username:** Try logging in via the email address or phone number associated with your account.

Make sure you're using the latest version of the **instagram app** to avoid compatibility issues.

*Key Takeaway: Common login issues can be resolved with password resets, two-factor authentication management, and contacting Instagram support.*
