## What is the Copa del Rey?

The **copa del rey** (King's Cup) is Spain's annual football cup competition. It's a knockout tournament where teams from all levels of the Spanish football pyramid compete for the coveted trophy. From the giants of La Liga to the humble clubs of the lower divisions, everyone gets a shot at glory.

But **why is copa del rey important**? Because it offers smaller teams a chance to test themselves against the best and potentially cause massive upsets. These David vs. Goliath matchups are what make the Copa del Rey so unpredictable and exciting.
